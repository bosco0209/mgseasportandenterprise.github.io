About MG SEA SPORT AND ENTERPRISE

Your trusted source for premium fishing gear in Mersing, Johor.

Our Story

Founded in 2000, MG SEA SPORT AND ENTERPRISE is a family-owned business dedicated to providing anglers with the highest quality fishing equipment. Located in the heart of Mersing, Johor, we have grown from a small local shop to a trusted name in the fishing community, offering a wide range of rods, reels, tackle, and accessories both in-store and online.

Our mission is to empower every angler, from beginners to seasoned pros, with the gear and knowledge needed to make every fishing trip unforgettable. We pride ourselves on our expert staff, carefully curated products, and commitment to customer satisfaction.
